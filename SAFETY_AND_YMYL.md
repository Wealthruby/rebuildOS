Safety Positioning: 
RebuildOS is a self-directed reflection and rebuilding tool.
It is not:
•	therapy
•	medical advice
•	diagnosis
•	addiction treatment
•	crisis support
•	a replacement for qualified help

Required Disclaimer
Use this disclaimer in the plugin footer, onboarding screen, and admin settings:
RebuildOS is a self-directed reflection and rebuilding tool. It is not therapy, medical advice, diagnosis, or crisis support. If you feel unable to control your behavior, feel unsafe, or are in severe distress, consider reaching out to a qualified professional, a trusted support person, or local emergency/crisis support.

Claims Not Allowed
The plugin must never claim to:
•	cure addiction
•	treat addiction
•	diagnose addiction
•	prevent relapse permanently
•	rewire the brain in a guaranteed way
•	replace therapy
•	guarantee recovery
•	solve mental illness

Safe Language
Allowed:
•	self-reflection
•	pattern tracking
•	rebuilding support
•	private journal
•	relapse prevention planning
•	accountability aid
•	personal system builder
•	high-risk moment
•	system adjustment
•	next clean action
Avoid:
•	addict label unless user chooses it for themselves
•	shame-based language
•	graphic sexual detail
•	fear-based language
•	moral panic
•	medical or clinical certainty

Distress Support Language
When a user logs repeated relapse, very high distress, or writes notes indicating they feel unsafe, show a gentle support prompt:
This may be bigger than a tracking tool today. Consider reaching out to a trusted person, a qualified professional, or local support service. You do not have to carry this alone.

Do not attempt to provide therapy.
Do not attempt to diagnose.
Do not attempt to handle crisis support inside the tool.
